Ride safely

BikeVision One offers increased safety while riding your bike. It is designed for all purposes: commuting, road biking, leisure riding, ... The bright rear light makes you very visible, both during the day and at night

Powered by technology

One is powered by an ESP32 microcontroller. This powerful board is the brain of the product. It offers a great feature set for us to develop amazing new features. The board offers WiFi and Bluetooth connectivity, this enables us to send over the air updates to keep your One fresh with new features.

Eyes in the back of your head

One also uses an accurate sensor that detects cyclist or cars up to 5 meters away. This sensor is very versatile. In a future software update, we will enable sensitivity adjustments. With this feature, you can adjust One to your liking. When the sensor detects a danger coming from behind, you will be notified by the vibration motors and powerful LEDs in the bike’s handlebars.

Ease of use

When you are done riding, you can easily take off One to make sure it doesn’t get stolen. One can be mounted on the included base. You can charge the battery by plugging in a Micro USB cable in the Micro USB port located at the back of the product.

Ride all year long

One is designed to be waterproof. This means you can ride it all year long. No need to worry about rain or snow.

Safety for everyone.

Most of our competitors require extra hardware for their radar systems to work optimally. These bundles get expensive quickly.

BikeVision One does not require extra hardware, as it is fully integrated in your bike. The vibration motors and LEDs are integrated in the handlebars.

We developed One to make safety equipment accessible for everyone. We are thrilled to announce that One will start at €125. We also offer an *installation service priced at €25.

*Installation service is only available at our Antwerp HQ

BikeVision One kit will be delivered with with all necessary parts and an installation manual. Our product is designed to be universal so there is no need to worry about fitment issues. Of course, we are always available to help through our customer service team
